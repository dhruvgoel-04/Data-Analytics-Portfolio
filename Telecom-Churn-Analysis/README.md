# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company to identify the key factors driving customer attrition and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

Using Python, Excel, and Power BI, the analysis explores customer demographics, tenure patterns, contract types, services used, and payment methods to uncover meaningful business insights.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and customer lifetime value.

The objective of this analysis was to:

- Identify the primary drivers of churn
- Understand high-risk customer segments
- Discover retention opportunities
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Excel
- Power BI

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Early Customer Onboarding

Focus retention programs during the first 1–3 months when churn risk is highest.

### 2. Promote Long-Term Contracts

Offer incentives and discounts for annual contract adoption.

### 3. Bundle Support Services

Encourage adoption of:

- Online Security
- Tech Support
- Online Backup
- Device Protection

### 4. Target High-Risk Segments

Prioritize:

- Senior Citizens
- Fiber Optic Users
- Electronic Check Customers

### 5. Improve Fiber Service Experience

Investigate service quality and customer expectations for Fiber Optic users.

- Payment behavior

The findings provide actionable strategies that can help reduce churn, improve retention, and increase customer lifetime value.
